The Legend of

Chapter IThe Abyss

Long ago, a great stone fortress stood watch over a vast abyss. The Dungeon of Ash, they called it. Its walls were built not to keep invaders out — but to keep something far worse from escaping.

As centuries passed, the fortress crumbled. Soldiers deserted their posts. Kings forgot their oaths. The ash rose and swallowed the lower halls whole.

Only the stories remained. Of lost treasures. Forgotten kings. Cursed relics. Ancient secrets buried under a thousand feet of dark stone.

Stories that drew fortune-seekers, mercenaries, and fools from every corner of the realm.

Chapter IIBarnaby the Bruiser

Among those drawn to the abyss was a retired warrior named Barnaby — a man who had survived more battles than he cared to remember, and drunk more ale than anyone thought possible.

Where others saw a cursed ruin, Barnaby saw opportunity. He claimed the crumbling walls above the dungeon entrance and set to work with hammer and stubbornness.

A place to rest. A place to drink. A place to prepare for the descent.

Barnaby grows older with every passing season. Some say he seeks a worthy successor. Others claim he knows far more about the dungeon than he admits.

Chapter IIIThe Broken Flagon

During the tavern's first week of business, a drunken brawl erupted between a crew of dwarven miners and a band of human sellswords.

Flying tankards. Broken chairs. Shattered pride. The common room became a battlefield.

At the height of the chaos, a stray warhammer smashed the tavern's prized ceramic flagon into a hundred pieces. Ale flooded the floor. Patrons cheered. Barnaby laughed until tears streamed down his face.

Declaring the destruction a lucky omen, he mounted the shattered remains above the hearth. The name came to him that very night:

The Broken Flagon.

Chapter IVYour Story

Today the tavern stands as the last warm place before the darkness. The fires roar. The bard sings. The brewmaster pours another round.

Beneath the cellar floor rests a massive iron grate. Beyond it lies the Dungeon of Ash — a labyrinth of monsters, forgotten kings, cursed relics, and ancient secrets.

Every adventurer faces the same choice. Every adventurer believes they will be different.

Few are right.

Somewhere beneath the tavern, something ancient has awakened.

The cellar grate stands open.

The fire behind you is warm.

The darkness ahead is not.

What will you do?
