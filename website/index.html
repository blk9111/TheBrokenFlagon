What awaits below

A roguelike with a home to return to

Every run begins and ends at The Broken Flagon. Die in the ash and you lose your gear — but your gold, your fame, your renown, and your legend all carry forward.

🏰

A Living Tavern Hub

Floor 0 is home. Eleven NPCs — innkeeper, blacksmith, banker, bard, gambler and more — react to your deeds as your Renown climbs through 15 unlocks.

⚔️

Tactical Turn Combat

Grid-based, turn-by-turn fights. Read enemy telegraphs, exploit elemental weaknesses, manage status effects, and time your class ability.

🎭

12 Distinct Playstyles

Four classes, each branching into three subclasses — from the death-defying Berserker to the minion-raising Necromancer.

🩸

The Pit — Arena

Survive Floor 20 to earn your place. Fight a fame-ranked champion roster across four seasons, capture dungeon beasts, and bet on yourself.

🗺️

An Overland Realm

Step beyond the courtyard into a 5×5 world of forests, roads, the walled city of Bravehold, and the impassable mountains.

🎲

Casino, Relics & Seeds

Roll the Flagon Dice, attune powerful relics, and share any run with a 7-character seed code — the same code always builds the same dungeon.

Choose your descent

Four classes. Twelve subclasses.

Every run starts with a choice that shapes everything that follows.

Warrior

120 HP · 12 ATK · 8 DEF

A frontline fighter who absorbs punishment and keeps swinging.

  • Berserker — fury without restraint
  • Knight — discipline forged into iron
  • Gladiator — the crowd demands blood

Rogue

90 HP · 11 ATK · 30% Crit

A skirmisher who strikes hard and disappears into shadow.

  • Assassin — one strike is all it takes
  • Trickster — outwit before you outfight
  • Shadow — darkness is the sharpest blade

Mage

70 HP · 9 ATK · 10 Mana

A glass cannon wielding ranged magic and careful mana.

  • Elementalist — command primal forces
  • Illusionist — nothing is as it seems
  • Necromancer — death is a resource

Cleric

100 HP · 10 ATK · 8 Mana

A balanced fighter blending healing with holy wrath.

  • War Domain — battle-blessed, unyielding
  • Light Domain — purity through darkness
  • Twilight Domain — guardian of the dark

Floor 0 · Your home base

The Broken Flagon Tavern

The last warm place before the ash. Between runs, walk the hall and trade with its vendors — every face has a purpose, and the room remembers you.

  • Bank your gold so death can't take it
  • Upgrade gear at the Blacksmith, train permanent stat boosts
  • Earn Tavern Renown across 15 living milestones
  • Hear the Bard sing songs about your real deeds

Survive Floor 20 to enter

The Pit — Arena

Bards write songs and merchants pay for seats. Climb from an unknown challenger to an undying Legend across four fame-ranked seasons — Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Champion.

  • Battle a roster of named champions, each with a rivalry record
  • Capture weakened dungeon beasts and fight them for your own
  • Bet gold on yourself — odds rise with your fame

Beyond the courtyard

The Overland Realm

A 5×5 world surrounds the tavern. Travel on foot across forests and roads to the walled city of Bravehold, forage for herbs, trade with travelling merchants, and survive roadside ambushes.

  • Visit Bravehold's General Store, Temple, and Alchemist
  • Forage wild herbs, ash berries, and glowcap mushrooms
  • Toggle the minimap (M) to chart every zone

Trust nothing on the deep floors

The Dungeon of Ash

One hundred procedurally-generated floors across four named regions — the Ashen Crypt, the Forgotten Mines, the Sunken Cathedral, and the Frost Peaks. Each enemy has its own AI, lore, and tell.
